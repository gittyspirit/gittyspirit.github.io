The Hard Time

It’s a kind of hard time to me. I don’t have too
much energy to solve all the problems. Yes, it’s impossible for me to have two whole busy weeks without any rest!

I feel little bit tired. Although I don’t have to spend much time on physical sports, but I need to join another competition that is not that challenging, but I’m afraid that I’ll lose too much. It has been a long time that I didn’t participate any chess competition. When I try to play with other opponents on the Internet, it seemed like that I can’t win easily as before. And I usually find that even I can’t beat noobs.

What I need is just a good rest like a puppy, crazy competing is not my
style.

I want to sleep like a silent puppy.
